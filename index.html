in plain language

Smart document fetch
Ask for any document — marksheet, Aadhaar, PAN, driving licence — and get a secure download link instantly.
🌐
Truly multilingual
Ask in Hindi, Kannada, Telugu, or English. DigiDoc understands you and replies in your language.
